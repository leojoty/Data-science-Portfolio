# Contributor Code of Conduct

## **Our Commitment**

We are dedicated to fostering an inclusive, welcoming, and respectful community for everyone involved in this project. Whether you're a first-time contributor, a seasoned open-source developer, or a user of this repository, we are committed to creating an environment where all individuals feel valued and respected.

---

## **Scope**

This Code of Conduct applies to all contributions and interactions within the repository, its communication channels (e.g., GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions), and external forums associated with this project.

---

## **Our Standards**

We expect all contributors to adhere to the following standards:

### **Respectful Communication**
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Provide constructive feedback and avoid personal attacks or harassment.

### **Collaboration**
- Assume good intentions from other contributors.
- Focus on ideas and solutions, not individuals or conflicts.
- Offer support and mentorship to new contributors when possible.

### **Integrity**
- Acknowledge and credit others for their contributions.
- Use the project resources responsibly and transparently.
- Address disagreements constructively and collaboratively.

### **Inclusivity**
- Avoid discriminatory, offensive, or inappropriate language or behavior.
- Ensure accessibility in your contributions (e.g., consider alternative text for images, clear documentation).

---

## **Unacceptable Behavior**

The following actions are not tolerated under any circumstances:
- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or exclusion based on race, gender, disability, sexual orientation, religion, or any other personal characteristics.
- Publishing private information (e.g., personal contact information) without explicit consent.
- Demeaning or disruptive comments or actions that interfere with constructive collaboration.

---

## **Reporting Issues**

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:
1. Please contact the project maintainers directly via [email address, e.g., Diversity@johnangie.org].
2. Provide detailed information about the incident, including links, screenshots, or other evidence if possible.
3. Reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly and confidentially.

---

## **Consequences**

Anyone found to be in violation of this Code of Conduct may face the following actions:
- **Warning**: A written notification outlining the behavior that violated the Code of Conduct.
- **Temporary Ban**: A suspension from the project for a specific period.
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from the project and its associated channels.
